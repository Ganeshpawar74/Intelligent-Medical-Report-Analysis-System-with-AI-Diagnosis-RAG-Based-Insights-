How it works

  1. 1
    Upload or type. A medical image, a typed report, or a list of symptoms.
  2. 2
    Our model predicts. Your trained Keras CNNs handle imaging; a Naïve-Bayes layer ranks candidate diseases for symptoms.
  3. 3
    RAG retrieves context. TF-IDF search over the disease KB and medical transcription dataset finds the most relevant passages.
  4. 4
    Gemini explains. The findings and context are turned into a friendly summary with recommendations and precautions.

Important

MediScope AI is an educational research prototype. It does not provide medical advice and is not a substitute for diagnosis or treatment from a licensed clinician. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please contact your local emergency services.
